Fractional CFO Services

Strategic financial leadership for growing businesses — without the full-time executive cost. Make confident, data-driven decisions with a seasoned CFO in your corner.

Book a Free Consultation

CFO-level insight, without the CFO salary

A Chief Financial Officer brings strategic clarity, financial discipline and growth-focused thinking to a business. But most SMEs can't justify — or afford — a full-time CFO. That's where our fractional CFO service comes in.

You get access to an experienced CFO on a part-time, flexible basis. We attend your leadership meetings, build your financial models, challenge your assumptions and help you grow with confidence.

Management Reporting

Timely, meaningful financial reports that tell you exactly where your business stands — and where it's headed.

  • Monthly P&L, balance sheet and cash flow statements
  • Variance analysis vs budget and prior year
  • Divisional and product profitability reporting
  • Executive dashboards with your key metrics
  • Board-ready financial packs

Budgeting & Forecasting

Stop guessing. We build robust financial models that give you a clear picture of future performance — and help you plan for every scenario.

  • Annual budget preparation and board approval
  • Rolling 12-month financial forecasts
  • Cash flow forecasting (13-week and annual)
  • Scenario modelling (best case / base case / worst case)
  • Capital expenditure planning

Strategic Advisory

Our CFOs go beyond the numbers. We help you think through major business decisions with financial rigour and commercial insight.

  • Growth strategy and expansion planning
  • Acquisition due diligence and financial modelling
  • Business sale preparation and valuation support
  • Funding strategy (debt, equity, grants)
  • KPI design and performance measurement frameworks

Finance Team Leadership

  • Bookkeeper and accounts team oversight
  • Financial process and internal control review
  • Finance team recruitment and onboarding support
  • Month-end and year-end close process management

Advisory Board Participation

  • Attendance at monthly or quarterly leadership/board meetings
  • Pre-meeting financial pack preparation
  • Independent financial perspective for stakeholders
  • Investor and lender relationship support
