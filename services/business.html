Business Systems & Process Optimisation

We map your workflows, remove inefficiencies and implement integrated systems that save time, reduce errors and scale with your business.

Book a Free Consultation

Work smarter, not harder

Most businesses grow faster than their systems. What worked when you had 3 staff falls apart at 15. Manual processes, disconnected software and unclear workflows create bottlenecks, errors and frustration — and cost you real money.

Maxima Accountants' business systems consulting service identifies exactly where your processes are breaking down and implements practical, sustainable solutions — from the right accounting software setup through to full workflow automation.

Process Mapping & Documentation

You can't improve what you can't see. We document your current workflows end-to-end, identify pain points and design optimised processes that your team can actually follow.

  • Current-state workflow mapping (accounts, payroll, admin)
  • Bottleneck and duplication identification
  • Future-state process design
  • Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) creation
  • Process documentation for staff training and handovers

Software Stack Review

Are you paying for tools that don't talk to each other? We audit your current software environment and recommend a connected, cost-effective stack suited to your business stage.

  • Current software audit and redundancy identification
  • Xero app ecosystem recommendations
  • CRM, project management and communication tool advice
  • Cost-benefit analysis of new tools

Workflow Automation

We automate repetitive tasks that are consuming your team's time — invoice chasing, data entry, report distribution and more — using platforms like Zapier and Make (formerly Integromat).

  • Identify automation candidates in your workflows
  • Zapier / Make automation build and testing
  • Xero + CRM + email platform integrations
  • Automated reporting and alert setup

Bookkeeping Systems Setup

For businesses setting up or cleaning up their financial operations, we implement a complete bookkeeping system — including Xero, receipt management, approval workflows and monthly close procedures.

  • Xero chart of accounts and tracking setup
  • Hubdoc / Dext receipt capture system
  • Accounts payable approval workflow
  • Month-end close checklist and calendar
