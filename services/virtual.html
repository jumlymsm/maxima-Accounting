Virtual Assistant Services

Dedicated remote administrative support to free up your time, reduce overheads and keep your business running smoothly — without the cost of a full-time hire.

Book a Free Consultation

Your remote right-hand person

Business owners and managers spend an enormous amount of time on tasks that don't require their direct attention — answering routine emails, entering data, scheduling meetings, preparing documents. Our Virtual Assistant service gives that time back to you.

Our VAs are professional, reliable and experienced in working with accounting and professional services businesses. They integrate seamlessly with your team and tools, operating as an extension of your business rather than a detached outsourced function.

Administrative Support

  • Email inbox management and triage
  • Calendar management and meeting scheduling
  • Travel and accommodation bookings
  • Document preparation and formatting
  • Online filing and folder organisation

Data Entry & Document Management

  • Invoice and receipt data entry into Xero or MYOB
  • CRM data updates and contact management
  • Spreadsheet management and reporting
  • Document scanning, naming and filing
  • Form completion and submission

Client & Customer Communications

  • Client follow-up emails and reminder messages
  • New client onboarding coordination
  • Survey and feedback distribution
  • Social media scheduling and basic content posting

Research & Reporting

  • Online research and competitor analysis
  • Presentation and report preparation (PowerPoint, Word)
  • Meeting minutes and action item tracking
  • Grant and tender opportunity research

How It Works

We match you with a dedicated VA based on your industry and requirements. You communicate via email, phone or your preferred tool (Slack, Teams, etc.). Hours are tracked transparently and rolled up in a monthly report.

  • Flexible hours — from 5 hrs/month to full-time equivalent
  • No lock-in contracts — scale up or down as needed
  • Fully covered by Maxima Accountants' professional standards
