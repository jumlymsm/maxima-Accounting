Accounting you can rely on, tax you can control

At Maxima Accountants, we believe tax should never be a surprise. Our qualified tax accountants work alongside you throughout the year — not just at tax time — to ensure your financials are accurate, your obligations are met, and your tax position is optimised.

We service sole traders, small businesses, companies, trusts and partnerships across all industries. Whether you're lodging your first tax return or managing a complex group structure, we have the expertise to get it right.

Business Financials & Tax Returns

We prepare fully compliant financial statements and income tax returns, giving you a clear picture of your financial performance and meeting all ATO lodgment deadlines.

  • Company, trust, partnership and sole trader tax returns
  • Profit & Loss statements and Balance Sheets
  • Depreciation schedules and asset registers
  • Division 7A loan account compliance
  • ATO correspondence and audit support

Tax Planning

We identify and implement legitimate tax minimisation strategies before the end of the financial year — so you keep more of what you earn.

  • Year-round proactive tax review meetings
  • Timing of income and deductions
  • Trust distribution planning and resolutions
  • Capital gains tax (CGT) management strategies
  • Superannuation contribution planning

Individual Tax Returns

From simple employee returns to complex investment income, we ensure every deduction is claimed and every dollar is accounted for.

  • Employee, rental property and investment income
  • Capital gains from shares and property
  • Cryptocurrency and digital asset income
  • Work-from-home expense claims
  • Prior year amendments and ATO dispute resolution

R&D Tax and Grants

Australia's R&D Tax Incentive can return up to 43.5 cents per dollar on eligible R&D spend. We manage the end-to-end claim process and identify grant opportunities.

  • R&D activity eligibility assessment
  • AusIndustry registration management
  • R&D expenditure documentation
  • Grant identification and application support

SMSF, FBT & Restructuring

We also provide specialist services including Self-Managed Super Fund accounting, Fringe Benefits Tax returns, and support for businesses under financial pressure.

  • SMSF setup, accounting and annual audit coordination
  • FBT return preparation and salary packaging review
  • Business restructuring advice and entity changes
  • Insolvency referral and support
