Payroll Services

End-to-end payroll management that keeps your employees paid accurately, on time and your business fully compliant with Australian employment law.

Book a Free Consultation

Payroll done right — every single time

Payroll is one of the most time-consuming and compliance-heavy tasks in any business. With Fair Work obligations, STP Phase 2 reporting, superannuation deadlines and award rate changes, getting it wrong is costly — both financially and to your team's trust.

Maxima Accountants takes payroll entirely off your plate. We process your payroll accurately, report to the ATO via Single Touch Payroll, manage super contributions and handle all leave calculations — so you can focus on running your business.

Payroll Processing

  • Weekly, fortnightly or monthly pay runs
  • Award and EBA rate compliance
  • Overtime, allowances and penalty rate calculations
  • Termination pay and redundancy calculations
  • Payslip distribution to employees

Single Touch Payroll (STP Phase 2)

STP Phase 2 requires more detailed payroll data to be reported to the ATO with every pay run. We ensure your payroll is fully STP Phase 2 compliant — including income type reporting, disaggregated gross, and cessation details.

  • Full STP Phase 2 setup and reporting
  • ATO finalisation at end of financial year
  • Payment summary distribution (or income statement notification)

Superannuation

Super Guarantee is currently 11.5% (rising to 12% in July 2025). Missing super payments attracts SGC penalties. We calculate, process and pay super on your behalf — on time, every quarter.

  • Superannuation Guarantee calculation
  • SuperStream-compliant super payments
  • Employee fund choice and onboarding
  • Salary sacrifice super arrangements

Leave Management

  • Annual leave, personal leave and long service leave accruals
  • Leave request processing and approval workflow setup
  • Leave liability reporting for financial statements

Employee Onboarding & Offboarding

  • New employee TFN declaration and super choice setup
  • Employee record creation in payroll system
  • Final pay and separation certificate on termination
