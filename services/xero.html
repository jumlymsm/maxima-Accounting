Xero Setup & Training

Full Xero implementation, data migration and hands-on staff training by Xero Certified Advisors — done right the first time.

Book a Free Consultation

Get the most out of Xero from day one

Xero is Australia's leading cloud accounting platform — but only when it's set up correctly. Many businesses switch to Xero only to find their chart of accounts is a mess, their bank feeds aren't reconciling, and their reports are meaningless. We fix that.

As Xero Certified Advisors, our team handles your entire Xero implementation — from initial setup through to staff training — so your team hits the ground running with a system that works for your business, not against it.

New Xero Setup

We configure Xero from scratch with settings optimised for your industry, business size and reporting requirements.

  • Xero subscription selection and account creation
  • Chart of accounts build — industry-specific and ATO-compliant
  • GST settings, financial year and tax rates configuration
  • Bank account connections and live bank feed setup
  • Invoice templates with your branding
  • Opening balance entry and reconciliation

Data Migration

Switching from MYOB, QuickBooks or a spreadsheet? We migrate your historical data cleanly so nothing is lost and your reports remain meaningful.

  • Historical transaction import and mapping
  • Customer and supplier contact migration
  • Fixed asset register transfer
  • Prior year comparison balance verification

App Integrations

Xero becomes even more powerful when connected to the right apps. We assess your workflows and connect the tools your business needs.

  • Hubdoc / Dext for automated receipt capture
  • Stripe, Square and PayPal reconciliation
  • Shopify / WooCommerce integration
  • Deputy, Employment Hero or KeyPay for payroll
  • Spotlight Reporting or Futurli for forecasting

Staff Training

A great system is only as good as the people using it. We deliver practical, jargon-free training tailored to each team member's role — from data entry staff to business owners.

  • One-on-one or group training sessions (in-person or online)
  • Role-specific training (bookkeeper, manager, owner)
  • Custom training guides for your Xero setup
  • Ongoing Q&A support for 30 days post-training
