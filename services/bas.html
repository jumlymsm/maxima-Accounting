Never miss a BAS deadline again

Your Business Activity Statement (BAS) is how you report and pay your GST, PAYG withholding, PAYG instalments and other tax obligations to the ATO. Getting it wrong — or late — can result in penalties, interest charges and unwanted ATO attention.

Maxima Accountants' registered BAS agents take full responsibility for your BAS preparation and lodgment. We reconcile your accounts, verify every figure and lodge on your behalf — giving you complete peace of mind.

What is a BAS?

A Business Activity Statement is a form submitted to the ATO — monthly, quarterly or annually — that reports:

  • Goods and Services Tax (GST) collected and paid
  • PAYG withholding (tax withheld from employee wages)
  • PAYG instalments (pre-payments of your income tax)
  • Fuel tax credits
  • Wine equalisation tax and luxury car tax (where applicable)

Our BAS Lodgment Process

We follow a thorough, structured process every lodgment period to ensure accuracy and compliance:

  • Review and reconcile all transactions for the period
  • Verify GST coding and correct any errors
  • Reconcile PAYG withholding to payroll records
  • Prepare and review the BAS summary with you
  • Lodge electronically via the ATO business portal
  • Provide you with a copy and payment summary

Monthly vs Quarterly BAS

Most small businesses lodge quarterly BAS. Larger businesses (turnover over $20 million) must lodge monthly. We manage both — and can request a lodgment concession or extension from the ATO when needed.
